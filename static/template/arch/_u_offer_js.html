Product description

Our 24% Lead Crystal Champagne Flutes are engraved with 'Mr' on one flute and 'Mrs' on the other with the Married Surname of the Bride and Groom and the date of their Special Day. A unique aspect of this gift is the pretty Love Heart Details that have been incorporated into the 'Mr' and 'Mrs' on each flute and the Love Heart Feature below the Wedding Date. This adds further quality and interest to what is already a special Wedding Gift. We also place a Silver Dedication Plaque on the lid of the Satin Lined Presentation Box for you to personalise further with your own personal message of up to 50 Characters - Use the Customize Now button before proceeding to Checkout to provide us with this information and we will do the rest! As Mr & Mrs for the first time on their Wedding Day, this gift idea is a special keepsake and one that the Bride and Groom will treasure for many years to come. The Personalised Crystal Champagne Flutes with Love Heart Details in their blue satin lined presentation box and your personal message to the Newly Wed Bride and Groom will truly make the perfect Wedding Day Gift.
